FILM PHOTOGRAPHY CLUB

Welcome to our vintage world in XXI century.
Hope you will enjoy your time here, settle in with your camera and stay with us for longer.

WHO ARE WE?

We are three individuals currently based in Copenhagen, but each of us come from different countries. Anna is from Denmark, Ana Sofia is Mexican and Gosia is from Poland. The love for film photography has connected us and we want to share our knowledge with others.

OUR VALUES

Our values are based around passion, tolerance and sharing knowledge. Our motto is to grow and make others grow. We are teachers but we are also students. We welcome everyone - we don't look at life through countries, languages or beliefes - we see our lives through camera lens.

OUR MISSION

We aim to gather all the individuals interested in film photography - teach the beginners or newbies and grow with the professioanls. We want to inspire each other to shoot better, try more stuff and step out of the comfort zone. We aim to provide our community the help and advice as well as analogue lovers family.

JOIN US!

If you would like to join our community, you are making the best decision in your film photography career. We will help you from choosing the camera to giving the people - the best camera brains, the biggest knowledge and also the nerdy sense of humour. Everything is free of charge - we just want everyone to share their ideas and wisdom with others.
